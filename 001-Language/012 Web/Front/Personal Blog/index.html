I am currently a postdoctoral researcher in Plant Pathology at the College of Plant Protection, China Agricultural University, working in the Biocontrol and Microecology of Plant Diseases Laboratory on AI for Science research. My primary focus is exploring biocontrol strategies for plant diseases using artificial intelligence technologies, molecular biology, and plant microecology. Additionally, we closely follow research advancements in the molecular genetics of microorganisms.
