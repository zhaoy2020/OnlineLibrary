Biography

Dr. Yanyan Lan is currently a professor at AIR, Tsinghua University. From July 2011 to February 2021, she worked as a professor at the Institute of Computing Technology, CAS. Her research fields include machine learning, natural language processing, and AI for Science. She has published 80 papers in major international AI and ML journals and conferences. Her work on Topic Modeling, Learning to Rank, and Neural IR has received several awards, including the Most Influential WWW Papers, Best Student Paper Award of SIGIR 2012, and the Best Paper Runner-Up Award of CIKM 2017.
